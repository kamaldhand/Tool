Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges. We may display ads to support the service, but there are no fees for compressing your images.

What image formats do you support?

We support all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. You can upload any of these formats and choose your preferred output format.

Is there a limit to file size or number of images?

You can upload up to 20 images at a time, with each image being no larger than 10MB. For best performance with multiple images, we recommend compressing them in batches.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What's the best quality setting to use?

For most web purposes, a quality setting between 70-85% provides excellent compression while maintaining good visual quality. We recommend trying different settings to find the perfect balance for your needs.
